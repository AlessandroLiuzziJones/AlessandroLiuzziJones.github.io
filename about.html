Hi, my name is Alessandro, a 3rd year Masters Mechanical Engineering student at Imperial College London. I am currently specialising in Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), Mechatronics, Embedded Systems(C++) and Statistics. Currently I am working on designing a unique watch for scaleable manufacturing and bringing static sculptures to life with mechatronics. With real world problems as numerous and complex as ever, it is my dream to continue to merge these disciplines in order to face these challenges head on. In addition to my work, I love to practice the guitar, play sports and read. I was born in the UK, however being half Italian I am fortunate to call both countries home and enjoy learning the language in my free time.
